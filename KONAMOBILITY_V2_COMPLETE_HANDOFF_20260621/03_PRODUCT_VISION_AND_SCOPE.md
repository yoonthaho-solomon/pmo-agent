# Product Vision And Scope

Generated: 2026-06-21

Business goal: increase call acceptance rate. Marketing can create more demand, but if calls are not accepted, demand leaks away. KONAMOBILITY V2 turns known callcard conditions and driver behavior into an explainable AI-priority dispatch candidate list.

In scope:
- Loaded data visibility
- 22D factor explanation
- Cosine similarity between callcards and drivers
- H3 origin/destination preference fit
- Top 10 candidate explanation
- Google Maps/Places/Routes as scenario and visibility aids

Out of scope:
- Live driver GPS
- Live driver online/vacant state
- Production dispatch execution
- ETA as a scoring component
- Production auth/rate limit finalization
